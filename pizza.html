Pepperoni Pizza

  • Ingredients:
  • Store bought dough
  • pepperoni
  • tomato sauce
  • cheese

Get the dough ready, mix the tomato sauce with a little bit of water, put sauce on the dough, cheese, pepperoni on top(the more the better), more cheese on top, some greens(optional), pop it in the oven and eyeball it to however ou like the crust fri your pizza.
